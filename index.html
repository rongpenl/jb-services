John's Profile

More about John

My introduction to the trades was a labourer for a masonry company in Ontario over 30 years ago, I realized that by being proactive and paying attention and working hard I could advance , I eventually was offered an apprentice position and enjoyed learning about the building industry . Since then I have worked for several top construction companies as a framer , lead hand , trim carpenter and eventually running my own residential and commercial property maintenance company

I have taken advantage of continuing education over the years as well , I have taken residential framing and forming courses through BCIT and property inspection courses and BC building code courses to stay current and able to serve my clients to the highest standards.
